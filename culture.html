Russia

Tolstoy                              Dostoevsky                           Tchaikovsky

Russian culture is a rich blend of Slavic traditions, Byzantine influences, and European modernization, known globally for its profound literature (Tolstoy, Dostoevsky), classical ballet,
music (Tchaikovsky, balalaika), hearty cuisine (borscht, pelmeni), and iconic art like Matryoshka dolls and Orthodox icons. Key aspects include deep respect for family, strong community
bonds, reserved public demeanor (infrequent smiling at strangers), valuing resilience, and a history shaped by vast geography, Orthodoxy, and periods of both isolation and Westernization.
